Our Story

Founded in 2024, 1103 Studio began as a small collective of creatives passionate about pushing the boundaries of design and storytelling. What started in a modest studio space has grown into a full-service creative agency, bringing together talented designers, developers, and artists who share a vision for creating exceptional digital experiences. Our journey has been defined by our commitment to innovation, attention to detail, and the belief that great design can transform brands and connect with audiences in meaningful ways. Today, we continue to challenge conventions and craft work that inspires.
